Reservation Policy

  • Reservation Period: Reserved books will be held for 7 days from the date of reservation. If the book is not collected within this period, the reservation will be automatically canceled.
  • Loan Duration: When reserving a book, members can choose a loan period ranging from 7 to 30 days. The return date will be based on the chosen loan period and is non-extendable.
  • Pickup: Reserved books can be picked up at the specified library location. Members must present their membership ID or reservation code when collecting their reserved books.
  • Multiple Reservations: Members can reserve up to 3 books at a time. If a reserved book becomes unavailable, members will be notified and may choose to reserve an alternative title.
